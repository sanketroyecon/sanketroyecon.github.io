Welcome

I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Economics, American University of Sharjah, UAE. Prior to joining American University of Sharjah, I completed my PhD in Economics from Cornell University, USA.

My primary research interest is in the field of Development Economics, more specifically issues on gender, health, women empowerment, domestic violence and economics of the household. I am also interested in topics in Political Economy and Labor Economics.

Thank you for visiting my website.
